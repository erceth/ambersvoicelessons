Amber has been singing for as long as she can remember. She has always had a love for musical theater but was frustrated that she could never land a lead role. At age 14, in an attempt to remedy this, she began taking lessons from a teacher who changed her musical life. Within weeks she discovered a strength in her voice she never knew she had, and in nine months landed her first leading role as Asaka in “Once on this Island.” As she continued progressing in her vocal abilities, she discovered a passion for teaching and a desire to share with others what the joy she had formed in her discovery of her own voice. Amber continues to work on her vocal and musical skills as she teaches. Amber has an amazing husband and beautiful twin girls. When she isn’t teaching and singing she enjoys cooking, playing the piano, and hanging with her family.
