Become the singer you have always wanted to be!

Are you looking to improve your vocal range? Are you trying out for a musical? Do you want to improve your performance in a band or sound better in your church choir? Whatever your singing goals are, I can help you reach them.

Welcome to Amber's Voice Lessons! With my instruction you will learn to sing by coming to understand your voice through personalized exercises, performance techniques, and songs you love that showcase your unique sound.

I am passionate about helping you reach your vocal goals. I have taught voice lessons for six years. In that time I have helped students get into choirs and lead roles in plays as well as gain the confidence to simply sing in karaoke bars with their friends. I am also a performer myself, focusing primarily on musical theater. I also have experience performing in Jazz Choirs, bluegrass and rock bands. I love just about every genre of music and I enjoy teaching you to sing in the style of your choice.
